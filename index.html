Luke Skywalker 1

Luke is a Jedi Knight in the Star Wars universe. He is among the last jedi in the Universe. He has a green lightsaber and can use the force. He is the one who defeated the Empire. He is from the Star Wars films that George Lucas created. He is the main hero from the films 4 to 6. The films are mostly centered around his father and his family.
