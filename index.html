Luke Skywalker

Luke is a Jedi Knight in the Star Wars universe. He is among the last jedi in the Star Wars Universe. He has a green lightsaber and can use the force. He is the one who defeated the Empire. He also has a wife and a child as well.

Luke Skywalker is from the Star Wars films that George Lucas created. He is the main hero from the films 4 to 6. The films are mostly centered around his father and his family. It is also speculated that he will reappear in the new Star Wars trilogy
